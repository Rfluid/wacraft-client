Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Wacraft ("we", "us", "our") operates a multitenant platform that enables businesses to connect to the official WhatsApp Business API. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our platform or interact with a business that uses Wacraft to communicate via WhatsApp.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you register for Wacraft, we collect your name, email address, and password. If you join a workspace, we also store your membership role and association with that workspace.

1.2 WhatsApp Business API Credentials

To connect your WhatsApp Business Account, you provide API credentials such as phone number IDs, business account IDs, and access tokens. These credentials are stored securely and used solely to operate the service on your behalf.

1.3 Message Data

When messages are sent or received through the WhatsApp Business API via our platform, we process message content, sender and recipient phone numbers, timestamps, and message status (sent, delivered, read). This data is processed to deliver the service and enable features such as automation and webhook forwarding.

1.4 Webhook & Automation Data

We process webhook payloads received from the WhatsApp Business API and any automation rules you configure on the platform, including trigger conditions and response templates.

1.5 Technical Data

We automatically collect IP addresses, browser type, and access logs when you use the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Wacraft platform
  • Send and receive WhatsApp messages on your behalf via the official Business API
  • Forward webhook events to your configured endpoints
  • Execute automation rules you have defined
  • Authenticate your identity and manage workspace access
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, and security alerts
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Meta / WhatsApp: Message data is transmitted to and from Meta's WhatsApp Business API servers as required to operate the messaging service.
  • Your Webhook Endpoints: If you configure webhooks, message and event data is forwarded to the URLs you specify.
  • Workspace Members: Other members of your workspace can access shared workspace data according to their assigned roles.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal process.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Message data is retained as necessary to provide the service and comply with our obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS), secure credential storage, and access controls. API tokens and sensitive credentials are stored encrypted. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Meta Platform Terms

Our platform operates in compliance with Meta's Platform Terms and the Meta Developer Policies. Data obtained from Meta's APIs is used solely to provide the services described in this policy and is not transferred to third parties except as described above.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@wacraft.app.
